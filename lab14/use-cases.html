Cybersecurity & Networking Practice

Safely test security ideas without touching production networks.

Conclusion

A homelab is more than just one computer. It is a platform for learning, experimenting, and preparing for future work in technology. Whether you are interested in virtualization, storage, self-hosting, or cybersecurity, a homelab gives you a safe place to try new ideas.
